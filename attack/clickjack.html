How this attack works

The orange button is bait. A transparent <iframe src="http://localhost:3000/admin"> is laid on top of it. When the victim is already logged in to LogiXair in another tab, clicking the bait actually clicks the admin dashboard's button at the same coordinates — destroying a station, deleting a user, or signing them out — without the victim ever seeing the dashboard.

Defense: X-Frame-Options: DENY and CSP frame-ancestors 'none' tell every browser to refuse rendering this iframe. Toggle CLICKJACK_PROTECTION in next.config.ts to flip between vulnerable and protected.
